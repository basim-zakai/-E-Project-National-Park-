Our Motive

Create a user-friendly web portal, "National Parks," designed for Windows using HTML5, JavaScript, and Geolocation. The site will provide enthusiasts with information on national parks and their wildlife. Unlike the current manual system, prone to damage and information loss, the proposed website allows global access to park details. It ensures compatibility with major browsers such as Chrome, IE, and Firefox. Simplifying data management, the site enhances user experience and eliminates the challenges associated with the existing manual approach.
