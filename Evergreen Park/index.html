About Us

Our History:

Through decades of dedication, the park flourished into a haven for eco-tourism, drawing adventurers and scholars worldwide. Its founding principles of conservation and education laid the groundwork for subsequent national parks globally. Northwood's legacy endures as an icon of environmental stewardship, inspiring generations to cherish and protect natural wonders.
